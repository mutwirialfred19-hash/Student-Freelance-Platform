Simple Process

How It Works

Getting started takes less than 5 minutes. Students use their official KCA email for instant verification.

1

Register with your KCA email

Use your official KCA University student email (studentnumber@students.kcau.ac.ke) to verify and join the platform instantly.

2

Build your profile & list your skills

Add your skills, portfolio, hourly rate and bio. The more detail you add, the more likely clients are to hire you.

3

Apply to projects or get discovered

Browse open projects and submit proposals, or let clients find you through the talent directory.

4

Deliver, earn & build your reputation

Complete quality work, collect payment, and earn reviews that build your freelancing reputation on campus.
